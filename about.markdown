<h2>About Me</h2>

I am broadly interested in using physics and computation to study chemical systems.

I am an undergraduate at the University of Maryland, where I study Biochemistry and Computer Science. I am currently researching molecular simulations with [Dr. Pratyush Tiwary](https://sites.google.com/site/pratyushtiwary/). I have previously worked with [Dr. Jeffrey Smith](https://irp.nih.gov/pi/jeffrey-smith) at the National Institutes of Health to develop simulations of networks of biological neurons.

For a more detailed description of my projects, you can take a look at my CV [here](/CV.pdf), or feel free to send me an email at pravin4094@gmail.com

Outside of research, I used to [speedsolve Rubik's cubes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Mz6WdI6FKzU), and I am an aspiring runner.
