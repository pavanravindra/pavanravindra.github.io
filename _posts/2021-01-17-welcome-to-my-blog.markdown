I've decided to start a blog for a few reasons:
1. It's always interesting to look back at my previous thoughts, and maintaining a blog is a nice way to document these thoughts for my future self.

2. Hopefully some of these posts will be useful for other students.

3. Blogs are a nice way to get to know somebody!

4. Writing blog posts seems like good practice for writing and organizing my thoughts.

5. I need a hobby that feels at least remotely productive.

I have a few blog posts planned already, so I'll be working on those over the next few days/weeks. These first few blog posts will be about my experiences with undergraduate research and the application process for PhD programs. I hope to eventually write blog posts that are more technical.
